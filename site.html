About the Site

Welcome to the website that is solely dedicated to Bread. In today's world there are many different types of bread all around the world. If you ever wonder where this miraculous food source originated from, then you came to the right place. In this site you will learn about the what bread actually is, how it was first discovered and how it came to be the common white, sliced loaves that you find in supermarkets today.
